Project Overview
A majority of the working population does not prefer the hybrid workplace as it decreases productivity. However, inevitably, the world is moving towards a digital workplace, and several HR leaders predict that most offices will become hybrid. Furthermore, with the digital revolution, AI products surface, and there is a decreased need for office workers in specific sectors. However, creative work cannot be automated.

Therefore, our team at Canon wanted to emphasize how our product would increase productivity and decrease overall frustrations with the hybrid workplace - specifically in creative office spaces.

During my internship over the past summer, I directed and led a product focused photoshoot, creating in-depth storyboards, shot lists, and set materials. The purpose of this photoshoot was to implement the pictures into future campaigns and an upcoming sales pitch. We mainly focused on translating our ideas into eye-catching images. Working with the public relations team, we identified main challenges of hybrid work, Canonâ€™s immersive solution, AMLOS, and the results of the solution.

Developing a Shotlist
In order to create the photoshoot, we needed a story.
My team and I created an idea that the basis of our photoshoot would be a camera company creating packaging ideas through a hybrid meeting to creatively collaborate.
Therefore, with this foundation, I created sketches in the perspective of package designers that we could implement in the staging background of the photoshoot, attending to every detail.

What we wanted to capture:
  1. How remote users felt excluded and unheard in team meetings
    • Users cannot see all the details needed in creative collaboration such as real-time sketches
    • These contribute to an overall decrease in productivity

  2. AMLOS' Main Features
    • Hand gesture spotlight technology
    • Multiple, real-time whiteboard zoom-ins
    • Presenter views

  3. The result of AMLOS for creative offices
    • Increased creative collaboration
    • Productive hybrid office work
